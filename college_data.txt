[UIET KURUKSHETRA - THE ULTIMATE MEGA KNOWLEDGE BASE & SENIOR GUIDE]

[1. COLLEGE OVERVIEW & HIGHLIGHTS]
- Name: University Institute of Engineering & Technology (UIET), Kurukshetra.
- Establishment: 2004. Government College affiliated with Kurukshetra University.
- Location: Kurukshetra, Haryana.
- Recognition: Approved by AICTE, Accredited by NAAC with an "A++" grade.
- Rankings (B.Tech Category): 
  * 2025: 188 out of 762 in India.
  * 2024: 174/284 (India Today), 100/160 (IIRF), 157/175.
- Top Recruiters: Infosys Ltd., JBM, 75way Technologies PVT. LTD., WaySpire, Beri Udyog (Fieldking), Airhome Stay, Academor Pvt. Ltd.
- Placements: Highest Package is ₹11.5 LPA. Average Package is ₹4.5 LPA.
- Scholarships: Har Chatravriti, Pragati, Single Girl Child, ONGC, NSP (PM-USP) Yojana (₹82,000/yr for 80% in 12th & income <= 4.5L), National Scholarship for PG Studies (UGC - ₹10,000/yr or ₹15,000/mo).

[2. EXHAUSTIVE FACILITIES & SERVICES]
- General Facilities: Wi-Fi campus, University Library, Students’ Support Services, Supporting International Students, Health Care, Career and Counseling Cell, Placement Cell, ICC (Internal Complaints Committee against Sexual Harassment), Co-curricular Activities, Sports, Dept of Youth and Cultural Affairs, NCC, NSS, SC/ST Cell.
- Mandatory Rule: Students must fill a specific mandatory form before applying for Character Certificate, 8th Sem Marksheet, Degree, backlog (if any), Caution Money, Migration, or Medium of Instruction.

[3. COURSES & ELIGIBILITY]
- B.Tech (4 Years): Eligibility is 10+2 with 45%. Selection via JEE Main.
- B.Tech Lateral Entry (3 Years): Eligibility is Diploma. Selection via OLET/LEET/HSTES.
- M.Tech (2 Years): Eligibility is Graduation with 50%. Selection via GATE.
- Ph.D. (Doctoral): Eligibility is Post Graduation with 60%. Selection via GATE or UGC-NET.

[4. DETAILED FEE STRUCTURES (2025-2026)]
- Important: You have to pay ₹55,400 fees of 1st year at the time of admission.
- B.Tech Total Fee: ₹4.2 Lakhs.
  * Sems 1, 2, 3, 4: ₹55,000 per semester.
  * Sems 5, 6, 7, 8: ₹50,000 per semester.
  * Branches: CSE, AI & ML, ECE, Electrical & Computer (EE), Mechanical, Biotechnology.
- B.Tech Lateral Entry Total Fee: Ranges from ₹3 Lakhs to ₹5.52 Lakhs.
  * CSE & Biotech Fees: Total ₹5.52 Lakhs (Sem 1: ₹50,400. Sems 2-6: ₹50,000/sem. Final year sems: ₹84,000 to ₹55,000).
  * ECE, Mechanical, AI/ML, EE Fees: Total ₹3 Lakhs. 
- M.Tech Total Fee: ₹1.49 Lakhs.
  * Sem 1: ₹43,820. Sems 2, 3, 4: ₹35,000 per semester.
  * Branches: Biotech, ECE, Defence Tech, Computer Engg, Production & Industrial, Electrical, Thermal.
- Ph.D. Total Fee: ₹3.89 Lakhs (Tuition ₹3.6 Lakhs + Dev Fee ₹9,000). Reg fee ₹20,000 for 1st year.

[5. HOSTEL DETAILS, FEES & STRICT RULES]
- Availability: Hostel is available for both Male and Female but seats are limited. Priority goes to 1st-year students, reserved categories, and those living farthest away.
- Total Hostel Fee: ₹12,840 per year.
- Exact Hostel Fee Breakdown: 
  * University Share: Room Rent ₹600, Water ₹240, Electricity ₹4800, Medical Fee ₹50.
  * Hostel Share: Utensils/Furniture ₹600, Dilapidation ₹360, Establishment ₹420, Common Room ₹720, Mess Dev ₹250, Ambulance ₹200, Tube light ₹300, Sanitation ₹150, ID Card ₹150, Mess Advance (Refundable) ₹3000, Hostel Security (Refundable) ₹1000.
- Mess Rules: Approx ₹2,500–₹3,500/month. Compulsory. Maximum rebate allowed is only 8 days per month. Food is average, like most govt colleges.
- Permitted Electronics (Slip Required): Cooler (₹400/month), Iron (₹400/month), Electric Kettle (₹200/month).
- STRICTLY BANNED: Heaters, inductions. Fine imposed if found. Smoking and drinking are strictly prohibited.
- Outsider Rule: Outsiders are strictly prohibited. If found, a straight fine of ₹10,000 will be imposed and room will be locked.
- Curfews: Entry timings are restricted and monitored, especially for girls' hostels.

[6. ADMISSION PROCESS (B.TECH)]
1. Eligibility: 12th PCM, appear in JEE Main.
2. Apply: Register on HSTES portal. Fill UIET KUK and branches.
3. Allotment: Based on JEE rank, category, preferences.
4. Acceptance: Pay seat fee to freeze/upgrade.
5. Verification & Reporting: Visit campus with 10th/12th marks, JEE scorecard, category cert, domicile. Pay fee, enroll.

[7. PHYSICAL COUNSELLING (SPOT ROUND)]
- Timing: Late (Aug-Sept) after all HSTES rounds. Only for vacant seats.
- Who can go: Anyone with JEE rank. Must report physically to campus with all original docs.
- Reality Check: Seats given on-the-spot. Pay fees immediately or lose the seat. High competition; CSE/AI are rarely available. Mostly Civil, Mechanical, EE left.

[8. CUTOFF TRENDS (JEE MAIN / HSTES 2024-2025)]
- Note: Ranks drop significantly in later rounds. Lower ranks can still get seats depending on category.
- CSE: Gen All India ~94k-1.29L. Gen Home State ~90k-93k. (Drops to ~6.8L for some reserved categories).
- AI & ML: Gen All India ~1L-1.4L. Gen Home State ~95k-98k.
- ECE (Electronics): Gen All India ~1.38L-3.24L. Gen Home State ~1.39L-1.56L.
- EE (Electrical & Computer): Gen All India ~1.49L-3.97L. Gen Home State ~1.47L-1.68L.
- Mechanical: Gen All India ~2.24L-7.7L. Gen Home State ~1.98L-2.08L. (Drops to 14 Lakhs for SC D).
- Biotech: Gen All India ~3.29L-3.35L. Gen Home State ~2.96L-3.23L.
- Lateral Entry (HSTES): CSE closes ~94 to 208 rank. Biotech ~1134 rank. ECE ~1315 rank. TFW ~44 rank.

[9. SENIOR'S GROUND REALITY & FAQs (THE BRUTAL TRUTH)]
- Q: "Bhai UIET lena sahi rahega kya?" / "Is UIET worth it?"
- A: Agar budget kam hai aur government college chahiye toh le sakte ho. It is a good option for affordable education, but it is NOT a top-tier college. Expectations should be realistic. The college gives you the degree, but you MUST work hard on your own skills. 
- Q: "How are the placements really?"
- A: Placements are average overall. CSE and related branches perform better. Infrastructure is decent but not comparable to top private institutes. Opportunities depend a lot on self-effort. Students need to work on coding and skills independently.
- Q: "How is the hostel and mess?"
- A: Hostel is available but limited. Rooms are basic with standard facilities. Mess food is average like most government colleges.
- Q: "Can I get admission with a low rank?"
- A: Admission depends heavily on rank, category, and seat availability. Lower ranks can absolutely still get branches in later rounds or the physical spot round.
